Greenland Hall Secondary School, Surulere, Lagos

Greenland Hall Secondary School, is owned and operated by a private owned company, Nigeria Province. The institution was founded 8th January,1998. Since its inception, Greenland Hall Secondary School has had the priviledge of ranking among the best Secondary (High) Schools in Nigeria. The school has a reputation for providing sound quality education to its students which prepares them for 'the real world' outside the high school walls. The noble institution, since it started in 1998, has turned out over 560 graduates who are currently making their mark globally. Students and alumnae of the Greenland Hall Secondary School are addressed as 'Great LOUIZANS '. This term usually gives a feeling of belonging to both the addresser and the addressee. Every single day we strive hard to achieve our mission - moulding future men and women of INTEGRITY.
